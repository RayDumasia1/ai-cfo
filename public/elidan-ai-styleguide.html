05

Patterns & Usage

Recurring layout and interaction patterns that keep the product feeling coherent across pages.

Soft Gold — usage rule

Gold is a signal of premium distinction, not a decorative colour. It appears at most once per screen composition. Approved uses:

  • The diagonal cap line in the logo mark
  • A top-border accent on a pricing or plan card
  • A "Premium" or "Enterprise" badge
  • A chart annotation marking an exceptional data point
AI-generated content

All AI-generated insights are visually distinguished from user data with a left teal border and an "AI" badge. This builds trust and transparency.

AI Insight Generated 4 min ago

Your marketing spend dropped 21% this month while revenue held steady. This suggests your organic growth is outperforming paid. Consider reallocating budget to product-led channels.

Website vs platform — colour application
Marketing website
  • Background: Cloud White (#F4F7FA)
  • Large Navy hero sections
  • Teal for CTAs and key highlights
  • Gold used once (hero or pricing)
  • High whitespace, large type scale
Platform UI
  • Background: Cloud White (#F4F7FA)
  • Navy sidebar, white content area
  • Teal for active states, focus, AI indicators
  • Gold for premium tier signalling only
  • Denser layout, smaller type, data tables

06

Motion & Interaction

Motion is functional, not decorative. It communicates state change and maintains spatial orientation. All durations are short — this is a professional tool.

--duration-fast
100ms · ease-out
Hover states, focus rings, badge colour changes
--duration-base
150ms · ease-out
Button press, input focus, sidebar item active
--duration-medium
250ms · ease-in-out
Card appear, tab switch, dropdown open
--duration-slow
400ms · ease-in-out
Page transitions, modal enter/exit
--duration-chart
600ms · ease-out
Chart bar grow on load, number count-up
Always respect prefers-reduced-motion. Wrap all non-essential animations in @media (prefers-reduced-motion: no-preference).
